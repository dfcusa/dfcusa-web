What is Design for Change?

DFC is a global movement that uses a simple design thinking framework to empower teachers and young people to do good.

How do I apply DFC in the classroom?

The DFC method is applied using a simple 4 step process based on Design Thinking, an iterative and human centered approach to solving problems used by the worldsâ€™ most innovative companies such as Apple, Google, Amazon and Facebook.
